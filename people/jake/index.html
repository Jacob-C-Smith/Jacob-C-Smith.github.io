Jake

I'm Jake. I'm 20 years old. My pronouns are he/they. I started writing the G10 game engine alone in early 2020. Programming is my passion, but I enjoy building knowledge in all types of subject matter. A few of my favorite are computer science, chemistry, philosophy, mathematics and socio-political economy. The development of the intellect is my greatest goal in life, because I was once told scientia potentia est.

Right now, my focus is on developing the G10 game engine. Once the engine meets my expectations, I'd like to apply the technology to create a video game, and begin to market it. This event will punctuate a shift in my labor, as I begin to focus on commercial products. All this is far in the future, of course.
